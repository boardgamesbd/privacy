Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fast Proxies for Telegram application (hereinafter referred to as the "App") collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Disclaimer: This App is NOT a VPN

The App is an auxiliary tool (utility) for managing MTProto proxy server configurations.

  • The App does not provide VPN (Virtual Private Network) services.
  • The App does not tunnel all device traffic.
  • The App serves exclusively to simplify the process of adding publicly available proxy settings to third-party messaging applications.

2. General Information

We are committed to maximum user anonymity. The App does not require registration and does not collect your personal data (name, phone number, or contacts).

3. Data Collection and Usage

3.1. Interaction with GitHub

The App loads the proxy server list from a public GitHub repository. The App requests data from raw.githubusercontent.com solely to retrieve a text file containing the server list. We do not collect your GitHub profile data and do not have access to your private repositories.

3.2. Firebase Services (Google)

We use Firebase (Google Inc.) to analyze the stability and performance of the App:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects anonymous crash and error reports to help us provide timely fixes.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects de-identified data on interface interaction (e.g., clicks on the proxy list).

3.3. Yandex Advertising Network (YAN)

The App displays advertisements from Yandex. YAN may use anonymous device identifiers (Advertising ID) to show relevant ads according to their own privacy policies.

4. Security

We use standard data protection methods provided by the Firebase and Yandex platforms. Since the App does not store personal information, the risk of personal data leakage is minimized.

5. Third-Party Data Transfer

We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties, except as necessary for the operation of the aforementioned SDKs (Google, Yandex) within the framework of their respective policies.

6. Consent

By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
